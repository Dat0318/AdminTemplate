How Marketing Leaders Flex to Keep Up with the Rapid Pace of Change

Ask any CMO what their biggest fear is, and most will tell you it’s keeping up with today’s relentless pace of change. But keeping up can be tricky as the two major drivers pushing change are the growing number of channels available, and the amount of data flooding in.

Neither driver shows any signs of slowing down. Because new channels will develop, you’ll have more formats to communicate with your customers, and you’ll gain more ways to gather data.

This means that, while everything may feel like it’s moving quickly now, it’ll only speed up.
But with new channels come new challenges. According to Forrester Research, 32% of marketers say a major barrier to omnichannel implementation is limited staff skills.

So how does a CMO keep up with all these growing demands, overcome new barriers, and propel business growth?

Many are extending their team’s capabilities with flexible talent accessed through talent platforms such as Upwork.

Flexible is the new staffing solution

Flexible talent platforms are the modern staffing solution for businesses that want to keep pace with change while optimizing their budgets. Through Upwork’s platform, you can connect with a wide variety of marketing specialists, when you need them, and for as long as you need them.

Your teams can tap into a global talent pool of marketing experts from graphic designers when you need to refresh a brand, to data scientists when you want to draw impactful insights from multivariate tests.

Nasdaq adopted a flexible workforce to extend its marketing capabilities. It uses a hybrid workforce model where the social media team leads the creative vision and they contract talent to do the work, which allows them to scale as needed. Having experts as needed to build out its social media arm enabled Nasdaq to achieve award-winning differentiation and greater reach.

Anna Gonzalez, head of social media at Nasdaq, says, “By having fast access to a diverse group of flexible talent, we can give clients fresh, creative ideas and execute on projects quickly.” She partnered with Upwork Enterprise for its combination of services and large, global marketplace. Aided by Upwork Talent Services, Gonzalez finds talent within a week on average.

Master being a Jack of All Trades

In addition to all things marketing, today’s CMO must also be adept at finance, design, technology, and data analytics. Juggling all these skills can be challenging. Doing it all at today’s pace of change can be overwhelming. By maintaining solid strategy and a modern staffing solution that’s hyper-responsive to your needs, you’ll ride the wave and keep your business on track.
