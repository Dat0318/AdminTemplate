How Engineering Teams Can Iterate, Test, and Deploy Faster with Flexible Development Talent

The tech skills gap is real, and engineering teams face fierce competition to attract top talent. But that doesn’t mean development teams have to settle for missed opportunities, outdated legacy code, or slow lead times. Upwork can match DevOps with trusted development superstars skilled in the latest programming languages and platforms.

With flexible talent from the Upwork platform, you’ll modernize more than just your stack—you’ll revolutionize the way you get things done.

Build a bench of trusted engineering talent

With the world’s largest talent pool at your fingertips, resource limitations can be a thing of the past. With access to a wide range of trusted experts in everything from mobile dev to microservices, you can easily ramp up your DevOps capabilities to tackle that backlog—without sacrificing quality or security.

Top developers are easy to spot with total transparency into their skills and experience. Post a job within minutes and start projects within 2-3 days on average. It’s how Dental Sleep Solutions finds the specialized experts they need to keep up with their evolving technology stack. They’re able to quickly fill their need for specialized web developers while saving 50% on overall project costs.

On-demand support for one-off projects or ongoing work

Demands spike and scope changes, but that doesn’t have to mean downtime or delays. Skilled flexible talent can help you to respond quickly to business requests and market opportunities. When you make these specialists a part of your strategy, you’ll be able to push all of your product and engineering efforts further, faster.

It’s how Dental Sleep Solutions gets reliable support for projects that last several months or longer, without sacrificing quality.

Need to chase the sun with code pushes around the clock? Have a product in development that needs some back-up UI and UX design expertise? No matter what your development team has on its plate, flexible development talent can help you get it done.
